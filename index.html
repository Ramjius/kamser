Who We Are

KSWCSI is a not-for-profit, non-religious, and non-discriminatory entity, committed to serving individuals regardless of gender, race, or ethnicity. We stand as a beacon of hope and empowerment, striving towards a society where all individuals, especially women and youth, have the resources and opportunities to thrive.
